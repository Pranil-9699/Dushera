When Dussehra is celebrated

Dussehra is typically celebrated in the month of October, on the tenth day of the bright lunar fortnight (Shukla Paksha) in the Hindu calendar. The exact date of the festival varies from year to year, as the Hindu calendar is a lunar calendar.

In many parts of India, Dussehra is a public holiday, and people celebrate with grand puja (worship) ceremonies, processions, and cultural events. The festival is also celebrated in Nepal and other countries with a significant Hindu population.
